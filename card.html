Husky

Huskies are energetic and athletic. They usually have a thick double coat that can be gray, black, copper red, or white. Their eyes are typically blue, although they may also be brown, green, blue, yellow, or heterochromic.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 22 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 13 years
  • Habitat: Originated in Siberia

As they originate from regions which are cold and snowing, Siberian Huskies are covered with a thick fur coat which keep them protected against the extreme weather. However, this does not mean to say that these animals will not be able to withstand warm weather. This is because Siberian Huskies also have an undercoat which is used to protect their bodies.
